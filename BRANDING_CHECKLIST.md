## üìê Logo Usage Guidelines

### Minimum Sizes
- **Web:** 24px height minimum
- **Print:** 0.5 inches height minimum
- **Favicon:** 16x16px (already implemented)

### Clear Space
- Maintain clear space around logo equal to the height of the "W" in WIZJOCK
- Don't place logo too close to edges or other elements

### Don'ts
- ‚ùå Don't stretch or distort the logo
- ‚ùå Don't change the colors (except approved variations)
- ‚ùå Don't add effects (shadows, glows, etc.)
- ‚ùå Don't rotate the logo
- ‚ùå Don't place on busy backgrounds without proper contrast

### Do's
- ‚úÖ Use approved color variations
- ‚úÖ Maintain aspect ratio
- ‚úÖ Ensure good contrast with background
- ‚úÖ Use high-resolution files
- ‚úÖ Keep it simple and clean
